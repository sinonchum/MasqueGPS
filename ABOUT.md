# MasqueGPS - About This App

## Professional Mock Location Tool for Authorized QA & Development

MasqueGPS is a professional-grade mock location utility designed for developers, QA engineers, and authorized testing workflows. It allows you to simulate GPS coordinates on Android devices for testing location-dependent applications.

---

## ⚠️ PLEASE READ BEFORE PURCHASE

### Root Access Recommended

**MasqueGPS works best with Root access.** While a non-root mode (Shizuku) is available, it has significant limitations:

| Mode | Stability | Detection Risk | Recommendation |
|------|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| **Root** (Magisk/KernelSU/APatch) | ✅ Stable | Low | ✅ Recommended |
| **Shizuku** (Non-root) | ⚠️ Unstable | Higher | ❌ Not recommended for production |

**Why Root is strongly recommended:**
- Non-root (Shizuku) mode cannot disable hardware GPS
- Mocked location may be overridden by real GPS within minutes
- Some apps may detect mock location more easily in non-root mode
- Root mode provides stable, persistent mock location

**If your device is NOT rooted, we strongly advise against purchasing MasqueGPS.** The non-root experience may not meet your expectations.

## 🔒 Data Privacy & Security

### Your Data Stays on Your Device

MasqueGPS is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- **100% Local Processing**: All location data, settings, and history are stored exclusively on your device
- **No Developer Servers**: MasqueGPS does NOT connect to any external servers owned by the developer
- **No Data Collection**: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data or location information
- **No Analytics**: No tracking, telemetry, or analytics are embedded in the application
- **No Account Required**: No registration, login, or account creation is needed

### Network Usage

MasqueGPS requires internet permission **only** for the following optional feature:

- **Map Display**: The interactive map (OpenStreetMap) requires an internet connection to load map tiles
- **Coordinate Input**: You can enter latitude/longitude coordinates directly without any internet connection
- **Offline Mode**: Once coordinates are entered, all mock location functionality works completely offline

**In summary**: If you choose to input coordinates manually, MasqueGPS works entirely offline with zero network activity.

## Features

- **GPS Mock Location**: Simulate GPS coordinates for testing location-based apps
- **Multiple Input Methods**: Tap on map, search by place name, or enter coordinates directly
- **Route Simulation**: Simulate movement between two points at configurable speed
- **Multi-Language Support**: English, Chinese, French, Spanish, German, Japanese
- **Dark/Light Theme**: Automatic theme switching based on system settings
- **History**: Save and reuse frequently tested locations
- **Notification Controls**: Start/Stop mock location from the notification bar
