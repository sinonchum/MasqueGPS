Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2026

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MasqueGPS ("the Application," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the Application accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data Collection Summary

MasqueGPS does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data processing occurs entirely on your device.

Data Type Collected? Shared? Purpose
Personal identification No No N/A
Location data Processed locally only No Mock location simulation (on-device)
Usage analytics No No N/A
Crash reports No No N/A
Device information No No N/A
Account information No No N/A — no account required

3. Data Storage

All data generated by MasqueGPS is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This includes:

  • Saved locations and favorites: Stored in the app's private directory on your device
  • Location history: Stored locally and accessible only within the app
  • App settings and preferences: Stored in Android's SharedPreferences

This data is never transmitted off your device by the Application. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

4. Network Usage

MasqueGPS requests the INTERNET permission for a single purpose:

  • Map tile loading: The interactive map feature loads map tiles from OpenStreetMap servers. This is the only network activity performed by the Application.

Map tile requests may include your IP address and approximate location as part of standard HTTP communication with OpenStreetMap servers. MasqueGPS does not control OpenStreetMap's data practices. Please refer to the OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy for details.

Core functionality works fully offline. Manual coordinate entry, location simulation, and all other features operate without an internet connection.

5. Permissions Used

Permission Reason
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Required by Android to provide mock location functionality
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION Required for location services framework compatibility
ACCESS_MOCK_LOCATION Required to register as a mock location provider
INTERNET Map tile loading from OpenStreetMap only
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Required to maintain mock location in the background
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Optional: allows resuming mock location after device restart (root mode)

6. Third-Party Services

MasqueGPS does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

The only external service used is OpenStreetMap for map tile display, as described in Section 4.

7. Children's Privacy

MasqueGPS is designed for professional developers and QA engineers. The Application is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers (other than OpenStreetMap tile requests), the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using device-level encryption
  • Using a secure lock screen

9. Your Rights

Since MasqueGPS does not collect or store personal data on external servers, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. All data resides on your device and is under your full control. You may delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the Application.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

masquegps@gmail.com
